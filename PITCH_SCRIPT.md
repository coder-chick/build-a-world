# Build-A-World — 2-Minute Pitch Script
> ~270 words · spoken at natural pace ≈ 2:00

---

**[OPENING — 0:00–0:20]**

What if you could go from a product idea to a full cinematic launch kit — images, videos, marketing copy, and a social strategy — in under two minutes?

That's Build-A-World.

---

**[THE PROBLEM — 0:20–0:35]**

Right now, bringing a product concept to life takes weeks. Designers, photographers, videographers, copywriters, strategists — all siloed, all expensive, all slow.

Founders and teams don't have time for that, especially in the early stages when you just need to *see* if something is worth building.

---

**[THE SOLUTION — 0:35–1:05]**

Build-A-World is an AI product lifecycle engine. You type one sentence — your product idea — and a pipeline of specialised AI agents goes to work instantly.

First, a strategy agent writes your product brief: name, tagline, target user, key features, breakthrough innovation.

Then a visual agent generates three stunning product views — a hero shot, a knolling flatlay, and an exploded parts diagram — all rendered in your chosen creative style from nine curated directions.

At the same time, a video agent creates five cinematic prompts and kicks off real Seedance 2.0 video generation — hero videos, action footage, animated sequences, and interpolation reels.

Finally, a go-to-market agent produces your full launch kit: positioning, audience profile, A/B-tested social posts with predicted engagement, and a viral mechanic.

---

**[THE OUTPUT — 1:05–1:35]**

Everything lands in one dashboard — a living product world you can customise in real time. Change the material, switch the environment from day to night, regenerate any image or video, and download everything directly from the app.

It's not a mood board. It's a deployable launch artefact — ready to share, pitch, or post.

---

**[CLOSING — 1:35–2:00]**

Built by a team of three in 48 hours using Next.js, Google Gemini for images, Seedance 2.0 for video, Z.AI for reasoning, and Butterbase for persistence.

One prompt. One pipeline. A complete product world.

That's Build-A-World.

---

*Tip: rehearse at ~135 words/minute for a comfortable, confident delivery. The natural pause points between sections give you breathing room to stay under 2:00.*
